Siddeswari campus

This campus is deeply rooted to our promise to serve the people by providing a strong medium for higher education that is on par with the very best private universities of the country. Located in the culturally rich Siddeswari, the students can naturally approach the whole of Siddeswari as an extended part of the campus to learn and experience the beauty of university life.

Dhanmondi campus

This modern campus in Dhanmondi has all the amenities that a student may require. Located in Sat Masjid road, the place is surrounded by modernization alongside some of the most inspiring landmarks of the city. Students in this campus have the prospect to harness growth in more aspects than one thanks to their surroundings making the chance to study here an experience that is truly enriching
